1 Person, 7 Billion Impact

Living day by day in our busy lives, it is easy to feel immortal, untouchable, even ephemeral. What matter is it that we may throw a wrapper onto the street as we walk by. However, our isolated actions culminate and multiply in impact, spanning the world and cutting years off of our futures.

For better or worse, one person among the seven billion people inhabiting the Earth can make an impact.
